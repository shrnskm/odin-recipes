Best Lentil Soup

Description

Whether you‘re looking to lighten up your meals or warm up with a steaming bowl of soup, this healthy lentil soup recipe is for you! This soup is made with mostly pantry ingredients but includes hearty greens and a squeeze of lemon for bright, fresh flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow or white onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, pressed or minced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 large can (28 oz) diced tomatoes, lightly drained
  • 1 cup brown or greem lentils, picked over and rinsed
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt, more to taste
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup chopped fresh collard greens or kale, tough ribs removed
  • 1 to 2 tbsp lemon juice (1/2 to 1 medium lemon), to taste

Steps

  1. Warm the olive oil in a large Dutch oven or pot over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is simmering, add the chopped onion and carrot and cook, stirring often, until the onion has softened and is turning translucent, about 5 minutes
  3. Add the garlic, cumin, curry powder annd thyme. Cook until fragrant while stirrng constantly, about 30 seconds. Pour in the drained diced tomatoes and cook for a few minutes, stirring often, in order to enhance their flavo.
  4. Pour in lentils, broth and water. Add salt and red pepper flakes. Season generously with freshly ground black pepper. Raise heat and bring the mixture to a boil, then partially cover the pot and reduce the heat to maintain a gentle simmer. Cook for 2 to 30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender but still hold thier shape.
  5. Transfer 2 cups of the soup to a blender and puree until smooth. Pour the pureed soup back into the pot.
  6. Add the chopped greens and cook for 5 minutes, or until the greens have softened to your liking. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in lemon juice. Taste and season with more salt, pepper and/or lemon juice to your liking.
  7. Serve while hot. Leftovers will keep well for about 4 days in the refrigerator, or can be frozen for several months.
